Our Story

Accounting? Boring. Complicated. Stressful. (At least… that’s what everyone thinks).

But what if numbers weren’t silent rows in a spreadsheet? What if they could laugh, argue, throw parties, and tell you their secrets? That’s exactly why Coinfetti was born — not as a company, not as a newsletter at first, but as an imaginary friend for your coins.

In my head, coins were never just metal or digits. They were alive. They cheered when you saved them, sulked when you ignored them. They had stories worth telling.
